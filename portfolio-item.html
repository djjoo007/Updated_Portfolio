WorkDay Scheduler

Daily Planner

The Daily Planner displays the current date at top of calendar. Time Blocks are shown for standard business hours 9:00am - 5:00pm. Each timeblock is color coded to indicate whether the even is in the past, present and future. Events can be added in the text field and save button will store event in local storage.
